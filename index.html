Voluntary Protection Program

If you see or experience a "Near Miss", or if you would like more information, please use the appropriate link below to access the Group VPP SharePoint site.

Provide outstanding safety and health protection to employees, contractors and visitors, through solid management leadership and employee involvement. We are committed to attaining a world class occupational safety and health management system with the philosophy of the Occupational Safety and Health Administration's Voluntary Protection Program (VPP). We will continually strive to reduce workplace injuries and illnesses and to protect our valued employees, their families, and our community
